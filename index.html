Labella.js

Labels should be beautiful.

Long long ago, in a computer far far away, there was a timeline, with many points on it.
A young researcher tried to annonate all those points and struggled to place labels without overlaps.
He spent a lot of time trying different methods without success, until he tried the force.
Since then, whenever someone asks him how to label points on 1D space, he simply replies, "May the force label you."
